# Executive memo — canonical shape

One page. Conclusion first. No warmup.

```
TO: [name or role]
FROM: [team]
DATE: 2026-04-20
RE: [subject — 8 words max]

## Bottom line

[One sentence: what we recommend or report. The reader should be able to
stop here and still know the right thing.]

## Why

1. [One-sentence reason.]
2. [One-sentence reason.]
3. [One-sentence reason, optional.]

## What it costs

[Time, money, risk. One sentence each. Be specific — "$120K one-time +
$8K/mo", not "meaningful investment".]

## What we need from you

[Specific ask. A decision by [date], approval to spend $N, introduction to
[name]. Make it easy to say yes or no in one reply.]
```

Never exceed one page. If the analysis needs more, attach it as an appendix
and keep the memo itself one page.
